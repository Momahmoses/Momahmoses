## 👨🏿‍💻 About Me

I am a **Geospatial AI Engineer and Data Scientist** based in Lagos, Nigeria, specialising in building production-ready machine learning systems and geospatial intelligence platforms that address Africa's most critical development challenges.

My work spans the full data-to-decision pipeline — from satellite imagery ingestion and PySpark ETL on Azure Databricks, through spatial analysis with GeoPandas and Folium, to deployed Streamlit dashboards used by planners and policymakers.

- 🛰️ **10 live GIS dashboards** — flood early warning, epidemic surveillance, conflict intelligence, urban planning, and more across Nigeria's 36 states and 774 LGAs
- 🏥 **Healthcare AI** — federated learning for cancer detection, malaria diagnosis at 97% accuracy on edge devices, maternal mortality risk scoring with SHAP explainability
- 💳 **FinTech & Credit Risk** — self-healing fraud detection with MLflow, SME loan default prediction, e-commerce pricing optimisation with causal inference
- ⚙️ **MLOps practitioner** — FastAPI microservices, Docker, MLflow experiment tracking, drift monitoring, shadow deployment, auto-rollback
